Design Principles

Key Principles

  • Visability
  • Consistency
  • Feedback
  • Constraints
  • Affordances
  • Accessibility

Cognitive Design Principles

  • Attention
  • Perception
  • Memory
  • Learning
  • Reading, speaking, and listening
  • Problem-solving, planning, reasoning, and decision-making

Accessibility Principles

  • Text to Speech
  • Large Font
  • Write clearly and consisly
  • Add pictures and icons for user recognition
  • Responsive to users issues and request

Conceptual Design Principles

  • Design interface to correlate with users knowledge
  • Make navigation clear and concise
  • More complexity means more options for the users
  • Use clear and simple writing
  • Complexity and lead to design trade-offs

Complexity Design Principles

  • Based on the user experience and preference
  • Translate to other languages
  • Watch for other further difference for different countries
  • Color apeals to different kinds of people
  • Shape of physical design
  • Use images to attract different genders

Cultural and Gender design Principles

  • Avoid complicated designs, create a simple and obvious design
  • Update model as the time goes on
  • Create a model with purpose
  • Created for interface applications
  • Stick to methods that are "used" and "trusted"
  • Use metaphors and analogies
  • Connect to how people are exposed through the product

Emotional Design Principles

  • Relies on emotional interactions with the product
  • Use users reaction to alter of advance design
  • Create and positive and stress free user enviornment
  • Communicate with users who may be fustrated to slove the issues
  • Convey emotions using emojis/icons, Sonifications and Vibrotactile
